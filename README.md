## Dashboard Objectives
- Track overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Analyze order status and shipping modes
- Study the impact of discounts and website clicks on sales

## Key Insights
- Local products generate the highest revenue
- Debit and Credit cards are the most used payment methods
- Free shipping is the most preferred delivery option
- Freepass subscription contributes the highest revenue

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends, helping businesses make better data-driven decisions.
